## 2024-03-07 - Empty States Call-to-Action Accessibility\n**Learning:** Buttons in empty states (like "Add your first transaction →") lack keyboard focus styles, making them invisible to keyboard navigation users.\n**Action:** Add `focus-visible:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-sky-400 focus-visible:ring-offset-2 focus-visible:ring-offset-slate-900 rounded` to these call-to-action buttons to ensure keyboard users can see when they are focused.
